Who We Are

AfroAtlas Limited is the first travel aggregator /consolidator in East and Central Africa, bringing cost-effective solutions for airlines, hotels, travel agencies and other travel stakeholders. Founded in 2019, AfroAtlas is transforming the way travel products and services are distributed.

Our comprehensive B2B functionality offers airlines, travel agencies and hotels the opportunity to better manage their sales and product distribution and to improve their overall offering as a B2B or B2C provider.

AfroAtlas reflects the perfect blend of global expertise and local/regional knowledge. This means we are not only using the latest technology for travel, but also customizing it to fit into the needs of the local businesses and tastes of local clients.

Where can we help you

Afro Atlas Solutions

Our comprehensive B2B functionality offers airlines, travel agencies, and hotels the opportunity to better manage their sales and product distribution and to improve their overall offering.

Flight Tickets

AfroAtlas provides access to local and international air tickets through its market leading system. This provides travel agents with up-to-date information on the latest airfares.

Hotel Booking

AfroAtlas provides access to the best hotel content of more than 1mn+ hotels and apartments on single platform. In addition to global inventories, we also sell local hotels/ apartments, which you cannot find elsewhere.

Airport Transfers /Meet & Greet

AfroAtlas offers a modern system and partnerships that will help travel agencies and hotels to book airport transfers and ‘Meet and Greet’ for their clients.

Activities/Tour Booking

AfroAtlas offers a digital system that allows you to sell activities/tours, such as sightseeing and other tourist attractions around the world.

Travel Insurance

In collaboration with reliable insurance companies, AfroAtlas can help travel agencies to arrange travel insurance for their clients.

Car Rentals

AfroAtlas supports the booking of car hire for any kind of trip. Through our platform, travel agencies can compare deals from the biggest car hire companies in the world.

Holidays

AfroAtlas offers a great selection of international holiday packages. The packages include savings on the flight, stays, room or meal plan upgrades and more.

Reservation & Departure Control System

AfroAtlas provides reservation and departure control systems to local and regional airlines, as we help airlines and airports automate all processes.
